求导公式及逆用自测

1. [f(x) · x^n] 一阶导数
\([f(x) \cdot x^n]'\)
\(x^{n-1}[xf'(x)+nf(x)]\)
\([f(x) \cdot x^n]' = x^{n-1}[xf'(x)+nf(x)]\)
2. [f(x) · e^{nx}] 一阶导数
\([f(x) \cdot e^{nx}]'\)
\(f'(x)+nf(x)\)
\([f(x) \cdot e^{nx}]' = e^{nx}[f'(x)+nf(x)]\)
3. [f(x) · e^{x^n}] 一阶导数
\([f(x) \cdot e^{x^n}]'\)
\(f'(x)+nx^{n-1}f(x)\)
\([f(x) \cdot e^{x^n}]' = e^{x^n}[f'(x)+nx^{n-1}f(x)]\)
